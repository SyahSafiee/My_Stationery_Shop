ABOUT US

Welcome to My Stationery Shop, your number one source for all things stationery. We're dedicated to giving you the very best of stationery, with a focus on quality, customer service, and uniqueness.

Founded in 2024 by Syahiran Safiee, My Stationery Shop has come a long way from its beginnings in a home office. We now serve customers all over the world, and are thrilled to be a part of the eco-friendly wing of the stationery industry.

We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
